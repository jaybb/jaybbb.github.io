SneakerMuseum

In the summer of 2013 I interned for SneakerMuseum, a Boston based start up focused on the heritage of sneaker culture. My responsibilities included managing social media accounts, designing web banners and posters for upcoming events, working with the social media marketing team to come up with giveaway contests, networking for the company at art events in Boston & New York, and photography.
